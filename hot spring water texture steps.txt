  -save and reopen the file

  -go to rgb
  -uncheck red
  -click 0,255
  -check red
  -click 129,255
-hue/saturation:
  -saturation to 120
-magic wand:
  -tolerance to 25%
  -click the parts that have an rgb of 0,255,255
  -invert the selection
-brightness/contrast:
  -contrast to 30
